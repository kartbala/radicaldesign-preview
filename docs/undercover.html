DESCRIPTION

About this product

The Undercover is the ideal transport cover for backpacks from 45 to 100+ liters and protects various types of luggage, from (bike) panniers to strollers. A disassembled Wheelie walker fits perfectly in size L, and a disassembled Cyclone fits perfectly in size XL. If you're flying with non-standardized luggage, using the Undercover is highly recommended. The cover minimizes the risk of your luggage getting stuck in the airport conveyor belts.

With its sturdy 8mm YKK zipper and lockable sliders, your belongings are safely stored. Additionally, the Undercover has a small eyelet on the zipper, allowing you to easily attach a lock for extra security. This is especially useful if your luggage is fragile or tends to get lost. Even on bus trips, you can sleep soundly knowing your belongings are well-protected.

What makes the Undercover even more attractive is its durability. Unlike single-use plastic wrap, which globally wastes billions of tons of plastic each year, the Undercover is made to last for years. This makes it an eco-friendly alternative for packing your luggage, while offering the same level of protection. And with a yellow Undercover, your luggage will be easy to spot among the black suitcases on the conveyor.



What size Undercover fits my backpack?
An Undercover that fits exactly around your backpack works the best but if you tend to do some shopping during your trip, it can be practical to have some extra space.
eg. A fully packed Pulsar 75 size L measures Ø117 x length 95cm and would just fit in an Undercover M. A size L Undercover would work too but leaves plenty of space. If your principal use is transport cover, go for the L, if used basically as rain and protective cover, go for M.
