About this product

Finally, our new Rack Bag is here! We’re incredibly happy with how it turned out, and we hope you’ll agree it was worth the wait.

The Rack Bag is available in two sizes:

  • S (16 liters)
  • L (28 liters) — featuring an extra pocket on the outside/front for quick and easy access to your essentials.

Each bag also comes with a rain cover for extra protection in all weather conditions. Optional are a side pocket and a bottle holder.

This product is size L.

It’s made from EPX200 fabric by Challenge Outdoor, which we chose for its excellent balance of durability, lightweight performance, and weather resistance. In addition, this fabric is produced using a more environmentally conscious process, making it a reliable and responsible choice for long-term use. Inside, the bag features an orange nylon liner, and at the bottom, it includes a honeycomb plate for added structure and protection.

We’ve selected a mix of basic and vibrant colors, so you can choose the one that fits your style best. For now, 3 basic colours are released, but soon there will be more!

The Rack Bag is compatible with multiple mounting systems: KLICKfix, MIK, Racktime, and AVS. Not sure which one you need? Check your bike rack — it’s usually indicated there. If you’re unsure, feel free to contact your local bike shop or send us a picture.

At checkout, simply let us know which connection system you need. We’ll install it for you before shipping, so when your bag arrives, you’re ready to go right away!
