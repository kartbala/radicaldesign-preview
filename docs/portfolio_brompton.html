Brompton

Since 2000 we are proud production partner of Brompton. Brompton is a well-known British bicycle manufacturer that specializes in folding bicycles. The brand is known for producing high-quality and innovative folding bikes that are designed for urban commuting and travel. Brompton bicycles are highly regarded for their compact and foldable design, which allows users to easily fold and carry the bike when not in use, making them a popular choice for city dwellers and commuters.
