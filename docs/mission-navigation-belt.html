DESCRIPTION

About this product

The Mission Navigation Belt is developed for the Netherlands Ministry of Defense in collaboration with Elitac Wearables. It enhances soldier navigation through haptic feedback, improving situational awareness in the field.

Mission

To create an intuitive and hands-free navigation solution that allows soldiers to stay focused on their surroundings while receiving precise waypoint guidance through touch-based feedback.

Our part

We contributed to the design and development of a lightweight, durable, and ergonomic belt using high-quality materials. Our focus was on ensuring seamless integration, waterproof durability, and adjustable sizing while maintaining precise haptic feedback.
