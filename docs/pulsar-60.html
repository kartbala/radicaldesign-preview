About this product

The Pulsar 60 is the slim sister of the Pulsar 75. With exactly the same features, but in a streamlined form, the Pulsar 60 is your partner for trips with regular provisioning possibilities. Using the expedition level comfort on this 60 liter pack is pure enjoyment. Featering two compartments, external front pocket and an extendable top compartment.

The Pulsars are custom backpacks, completely handmade in the Netherlands. The non-adjustable back in five sizes is indestructible, extremely stable and has an unparalleled comfort.

Available in green and orange, in 5 back sizes.
