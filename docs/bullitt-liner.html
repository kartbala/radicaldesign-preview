DESCRIPTION

About this product

With this additional liner, you can make your Bullitt Bag V2 waterproof. The liner is made of a 120D ripstop nylon with a durable PU coating. All the seams of the liner are taped to ensure no water can get in. The liner attaches with velcro to the Bullitt Bag V2 and has the exact same shape and dimensions, so there is no compromise on the way you use your Bullitt Bag. The liner is made of only 3 panels with a very light silver/grey colour. This make it easier to look for an take stuff out of the bag, as more light is reflected into the bag and it’s all one smooth surface.
