DESCRIPTION

About this product

The Rack Bag Large recumbent bag has 25 liters of space. It is not only padded, but has an internal frame to prevent it from sagging on narrow or short racks. This may also prevent the bag to cover the rear light on certain racks. To attach it to your recumbent, slide the front strap over your seat, place the bag on your rack, then attach and tension the two rack straps.

Available in 5 colours
