DESCRIPTION

About this product

Discover the Cyclone Trekking Dog Trailer from Radical Design, designed for adventurous cycling trips with your dog. This dog trailer offers ample space for a small to medium-sized dog of up to 20 kg, with internal dimensions of 88x43x65cm (LxWxH).

The Dog Trailer is built on the reliable frame of our fourth generation and multiple award-winning Cyclone Trekking bike trailer, making it modular for versatile use. Already own a Cyclone Trekking? Then you only need to purchase the separate dog trailer module and swap it out with your bag or cargo cover. The canopy and tub are largely made from Cordura, just like the original Cyclone Trekking bag, and we have naturally chosen the best zippers from YKK for this design as well. This makes it a lightweight (only 7 kg!) and robust radical product. Additionally, all parts are available separately.

What makes the Cyclone Dog Trailer so special? It is compact, foldable (easily fits under your bed when folded), and has a low entry for your four-legged friend. Use it as a bench (also for the car), complete with a supplied leash for securing your dog. Ventilation is possible through the front and back, and for extra visibility during the day and night, reflectors, reflective piping, and a lamp holder are added. An optional removable dog cushion is available.

The Cyclone Dog Trailer is equipped with a stainless steel hitch that fits almost any quick-release or rear axle. The trailer comes standard with one hitch, but with an extra hitch, the trailer can be quickly transferred to another bike equipped with this hitch.

Our dog trailer is also suitable for attaching to a 16-inch bike, for example, a Brompton folding bike. For this, we provide a shorter drawbar and a Brompton hitch. Indicate at checkout that you want this, and we will adjust the package accordingly!
