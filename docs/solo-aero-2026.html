About this product

Finally, our new Solo Aero is here — where we combine the old Solo Aero Wide ánd Small in one!

It’s made from EPX200 fabric by Challenge Outdoor, which we chose for its excellent balance of durability, lightweight performance, and weather resistance. In addition, this fabric is produced using a more environmentally conscious process, making it a reliable and responsible choice for long-term use. Inside, the bag features an orange nylon liner. Optional are a side pocket and a bottle holder.

For now we’ve selected basic colors: Black Knight, Coyote Brown and Wolf Grey. This bag suits most hardshell seats of 2-wheel recumbents. Not suitable for lowracers. Doubt if this bag will fit your bike? Contact us for more info!

It's always advisable to use Seat Edge Protector for hardshell seats.
