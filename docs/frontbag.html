DESCRIPTION

About this product

The most streamlined bag we have ever developed. The Velomobile Front Bag fits perfectly next to your feet, at the front of your velomobile. Designed for the Alpha, Milan, and Bülk series and the Tuna, which are perfectly suited for this model thanks to their easily accessible front cover. The inside is lined in orange and features two compartments.

With the supplied self-adhesive Velcro, you can easily attach the bag to the inside of your velomobile. Compact, sturdy, and cleverly designed – exactly what you need on the road. Thanks to the detachable shoulder strap, you can quickly transform the bag into a practical handbag.

Made from sage green Cordura with an orange lining!
