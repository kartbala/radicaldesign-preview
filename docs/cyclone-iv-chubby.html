DESCRIPTION

About this product

The Chubby Bicycle Trailer has been specially designed to travel with compact folding bikes. It uses exactly the same technology as our multi-prize-winning Cyclone Trekking and Cyclone Cargo Bicycle Trailers. This version of the Cyclone is wider and shorter than the regular Cyclone and has a black "padded" bag. The wheels of the Chubby have the same size as the wheels on your folding bike.

- Available in 3 versions: Brompton, Birdy and 26-29" bike. Each version has its own dedicated wheel size and hitch ball.
- The new Brompton G-line is too big to fit in the Chubby Trailer and the Brompton Backpack!
- When you want to put one of our trailers behind your Brompton G-Line Eletric, please add a Hitch Adapter for Bicycle-Trailer to make sure your hitch can be attached to your G-Line Electric!
- The hitch is equipped with an M10 hole. The new Brompton T/P/C line series have an M12 axle. We are working on a solution for this, but until then, you will need to slightly drill out the hitch (or note it with your order and we will do this for you!). We apologize for the inconvenience.



With the Chubby you can travel by plane, with your bike safely stored inside the padded Chubby bag. On arrival, just take out the bike, put your luggage in and connect the Chubby to your bike to have a comfortable travel set-up with 100 litres of storage.

Or how about picking up your friend at the railway station with a bike in the Chubby, for him or her to ride back home with you?

The Cyclone IV Chubby is a two-wheeler so it keeps itself in balance without affecting the behavior of the towing bike. With the tow bar connecting to the bike's rear hub, your bike's rear rack is still available for luggage.

At home the Chubby folds away real small, on the road it sports a capacity of 100 liters and 40 kg. The bag opens all around for maximum accessibility, using a 10mm YKK zipper with two lockable sliders. The TIG welded frame bushings guarantee a strong and secure connection of the wheels to the frame, even at high loads and speeds. The wheels are equipped with industrial closed bearings, ensuring minimal rolling resistance and maximum security.

When the wheels are inserted in the rear axle bushings, the Cyclone IV Chubby can be used as a walking trailer. This way you can easily cover considerable distances, even with fully loaded trailer.

When transporting the Chubby by train, tow-bar and wheels can be detached with a twist of the hand, which visually leaves only a big duffle bag. The tow-bar can easily be stored inside and the shoulder strap and the two big handles make it easy to lift.

The Cyclone IV Chubby comes with a rain cover. The Chubby rain cover is a lightweight solution to keep your trailer and its contents dry and clean. At only 150 grams, this small package will always find a place in the huge Cyclone bag. When a shower takes you by surprise, the cover is attached in seconds and securely velcroed around the tow-bar connector. Many people leave it on all day, no matter the weather, as its visibility and protection against dirt is just as much appreciated.

The hitch fits on virtually any bike, whether with dropout or fixed rear axle and is made of stainless steel. It can be ordered separately to make more bikes 'Cyclone-compatible'. The tow bar can be detatched in seconds. This requires only one hand, so the other hand can hold the bike.

Just like with all our products, the Cyclone comes with the assurance that all the materials used in it are also available for separate purchase. This means that if you have a minor mishap with the bicycle trailer, we can send you spare parts for repairs. Even if you're on the other side of the world, we can dispatch the necessary parts to you so you can quickly get back on track.
