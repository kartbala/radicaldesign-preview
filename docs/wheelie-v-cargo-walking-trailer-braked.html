About this product

The Wheelie V Cargo Walking Trailer – For Adventurers and Long-Distance Hikers

The Wheelie V Cargo is designed for maximum versatility and ease of use. This model is perfect for hikers who want to bring their own backpack and features an open base with sturdy straps and buckles to securely attach your gear. Like the Traveller, the Cargo can carry up to 40 kg and is suitable for long-distance trekking.

We’ve TIG-welded the axle sleeves and added an extra third crossbar to ensure a strong and stable frame. The hip belt is comfortable and quick to adjust for any user. Suitable for waist sizes from approximately 72 cm to 154 cm.

Features of the Wheelie V Cargo:

  • Open base for use with your own backpack.
  • Reinforced frame with TIG-welded components.
  • Hands-free use or compatible with trekking poles.
  • Excellent balance for minimal strain on your back and shoulders.
  • Durable solid tires and wheels with sealed industrial bearings.
  • Drum brakes on both wheels.

Spare Parts and Maintenance
As with all our products, the materials used in the Wheelie V Cargo are available as spare parts. Should you encounter an issue, we can ship the necessary components worldwide so you can quickly continue your journey.

Questions?
Do you have questions about this product or need help making a decision? Feel free to contact us!

This braked version is equipped with wheels featuring drum brakes and a single brake lever for the right hand, which controls both drum brakes simultaneously.
