DESCRIPTION

About this product

This is the latest version of the Weber/Radical Design Hitch, now including a lock.
It allows you to securely lock your Cyclone trailer to your bicycle.

The Weber Hitch makes it possible to use a Cyclone bicycle trailer when you already have a Weber coupling system installed on your bike.
For example, you can use a child trailer with the Weber hitch during the week and easily switch to your Cyclone trailer for a weekend trip — all without having to change the hitch on your bicycle.

Please note: you do need the corresponding Weber part on your bicycle (we do not sell this).
