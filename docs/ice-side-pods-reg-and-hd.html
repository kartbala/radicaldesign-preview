About this product

The ICE Side Pods are based on the Banana racer panniers of our own range. The pods have fixed suspension straps, optimised for either the regular or the HD ICE mesh seats. At the back they have a hook on each pannier, to connect them to the cross bar on the seat frame. They cannot be used for the ICE hard shell seats. The pods are made in Vietnam.

Available only through authorized ICE recumbent Trike dealers
