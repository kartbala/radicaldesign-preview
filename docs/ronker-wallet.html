DESCRIPTION

About this product

The Ronker Wallet, also known as Planck Wallet, is an innovative new wallet design that made the thinnest wallet possible. The intelligence is in the coin section where the coins are placed below the cards and in such a way that they stay as flat as possible. In all honesty we were quite skeptical when we were asked to help design and produce the wallet. However, after using the first prototypes for a whole year we are sure this is one of the best wallets ever made and we never want to go back to regular wallets. This Special Edition Ronker has the Radical Design logo on the front. (comes without content hihi…)
