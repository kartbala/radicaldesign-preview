DESCRIPTION

About this product

The velomobile parking cover is exactly what the name suggests. It protects your precious velomobile against dust and water, but also against prying eyes and hands of people passing by. Fully customized and handmade in our Dutch workshop for each velomobile.

We tried hard to find the best fabric able to compromise between strength, durability and water resistance, so please keep in mind this cover is not 100% waterproof (except of the new XL cover) and although the body can get wet it will keep water out of your velomobile. Because of the elastic cord in the bottom edge of the cover you only need to pull the cover over the nose and push it down across the tail. It then stays in place by itself. There are loops on both sides of the rear wheel through which a bike lock can be inserted.

Because of the choice for the material Honeycomb, this cover is not meant as a full year round out side parking cover. It is possible of course, but please keep in mind that this will wear out the cover faster.

The newest model (XL Cover) is the first model equipped with taped seams. For future productions, we will also make the other models waterproof in the same way.
