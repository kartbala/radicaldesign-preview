DESCRIPTION

About this product

Do you already have a Cyclone Trekking? Good news: this dog trailer module is compatible with your frame! Easily remove your Trekking bag and attach the dog module to your frame.

What makes the Cyclone Dog Trailer so special? It is compact, foldable (easily fits under your bed when folded), and has a low entry for your four-legged friend. Use it as a bench (also for the car), complete with a supplied leash for securing your dog. Ventilation is possible through the front and back, and for extra visibility during the day and night, reflectors, reflective piping, and a lamp holder are added. An optional removable dog cushion is available.

This product is not the full dog trailer, this is an accesory for customers who already have the Cyclone Trekking frame. Not sure which frame you have? Read our blog about our trailers here!

The reflector set is not included, you can order them seperatly here!
