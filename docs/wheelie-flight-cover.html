DESCRIPTION

About this product

The Wheelie Flight Cover is engineered to protect and transport your Wheelie during air, rail, or bus travel. The cover accommodates the Wheelie with the towbars, wheels, and hip belt stored inside, while allowing the luggage to remain mounted on the frame. When required, the wheels can be attached so the trailer can be towed manually using the handle. The double sliders can be closed with a lock (not included).

Reinforced Hypalon patches on the corners provide targeted abrasion and impact protection at critical contact points. During the design process, careful consideration was given to achieving an optimal balance between protection and added weight, ensuring effective safeguarding of the Wheelie without unnecessary bulk. For additional security, users can always add extra protective materials inside the cover according to their travel needs.
