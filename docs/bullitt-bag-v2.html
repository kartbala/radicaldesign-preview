DESCRIPTION

About this product

The Bullitt bag V2 is the new version of our transortbag made for the Bullitt Bike and also Omnium bikes. The bag is used by professional and private customers to transport a lot of stuff securely and protected from the weather. With up to 400 liter of volume, there isn’t much that won’t fit in this bag. When (half)empty, the bag folds down onto the bike or contents, so there is no unnecessary drag.

The bag is made to last. It's made of Cordura 1000, at some points reinforced with 1mm Hypalon, like the front panel. These materials are specially made for abrasion resistance. The bottom is reinforced with 5mm honeycomb board and a 7mm layer of foam. This allows you to use the Bullitt Bag V2 without any additional bottom plate installed on your cargo area. The foam dampens the vibration of the bike, so it’s silent and the contents of the bag aren’t damaged.

On the back of the bag we’ve placed 2 laser-cut MOLLE (PALS) webbing panels. This standardized fixation system allows you to customize your Bullitt Bag V2 in any way you like. For example using our Bullitt MOLLE Auxiliary pockets with quick attach system, but any other standard MOLLE (PALS) accessory will fit as well.

The Bullitt Bag V2 is the base model of the bag and can be expanded with the following products:
- Bullitt waterproof liner
- Bullitt Box insert
- Bullitt pocket
- Bullitt Mudguard
